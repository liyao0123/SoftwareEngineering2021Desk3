Game Instruction


There are two games for you to enhance impression of endangered fauna

1. Help Fauna Find Home

In order to protect endangered animals, scientists have brought them to a safe place to protect them.

Now that their populations have expanded, animals can return to their homes.

Can you help scientists find homes for endangered animals?

Review And Get Ready

2. Observation And Memory Test

There are many similar animals among the endangered animals.

For ordinary people, it is difficult to distinguish animals that belong to one group.

Can you find the difference between them?
